Our Story

Welcome to The Factory, Jaipur's favorite neighborhood bakery. We specialize in artisanal breads, decadent pastries, and custom cakes that bring people together.

Every morning, our bakers start before dawn to create fresh, handcrafted goods using traditional techniques and the finest ingredients. Whether you're stopping by for your daily bread or a special celebration cake, we promise quality in every bite.

Our bakery is designed to be welcoming to all - from children picking out their first cookie to seniors enjoying their favorite tea cake.
