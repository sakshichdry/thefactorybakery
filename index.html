Our Story

Welcome to The Factory, your neighborhood destination for freshly baked goods, delicious pizzas, and comforting meals in Jaipur.

Our bakers start before dawn to create artisan breads and pastries using traditional techniques. Our chefs prepare hearty meals with the same care and attention to quality.

At The Factory, we believe good food brings people together. Our space is designed to welcome everyone - from children enjoying their first cookie to seniors savoring their favorite tea cake.

What began as a small bakery has grown into a beloved cafe and food court, serving families, students, and food lovers of all ages.

Whether you're stopping by for your morning coffee and croissant, a lunchtime pizza, or an evening treat, we promise food made with love.
